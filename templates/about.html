About RoomieStatz

Finding a roommate can feel like a gamble. You're trusting someone you barely know with your home, your daily routine, and your peace of mind — often based on nothing more than a short conversation or a gut feeling.

RoomieStatz exists to change that. Our mission is simple: help people connect with roommates they can actually trust, so the search for a place to live feels a little less like a gamble and a little more like a genuine connection.

By letting real people share honest, rated experiences about what it's like to live with someone — cleanliness, noise habits, communication, and more — we're building a community where everyone can feel safer and more secure as they look for their next home.

Whether you're searching for a roommate for the first time or looking back on past living situations, RoomieStatz is here to help you make informed, confident decisions about who you choose to live with.

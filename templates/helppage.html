How To Get Started

  1. Create an account — click "Login" in the top navigation and sign up with your email.

  2. Fill out your bio — after signing up, you'll be guided to a short questionnaire covering things like cleanliness, noise preferences, and daily habits.

  3. Add a profile picture — visit your Bio page anytime and click the profile picture box to upload a photo.

  4. Rate a roommate — use the "Rate Your Roommate" link in the navigation to leave ratings for people you've lived with.

  5. Check your profile — your Bio page shows your average rating, combining your own answers and ratings from others.
